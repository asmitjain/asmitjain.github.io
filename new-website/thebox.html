The Mystery Box

An interactive arcade installation

A brainchild of three people who grew up breaking things apart who wanted to blend their love for the tactile interfaces with the sleek modern digital displays. Originally built as part of the Physical computation class at ITP by Arnab, Atharva & Lillian the box has since been part the ITP winter exhibit 2018 & Rogue's Gallery in 2019.
