ABOUT

I am a Mechanical Engineer from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst. I recently completed my Master of Science (MS) degree from the College of Engineering in May 2019. I am currently working as a Research & Development Engineer at Excel Dryer, Inc and part-time as an Additive Manufacturing & Design Engineer at ADDFab Lab in Life Sciences Laboratories - UMass Amherst.

I have a prior industry experience of over two years and research experience of over two year at top-notch labs in UMass. During my engineering career, I have worked on a variety of projects and have played a part in researching and developing new technologies, product quality improvements, cost reductions efforts for products, efficiency and productivity enhancement, and developing strong business relations with the customers, vendors and suppliers.

I have honed my skills in Product and Process Design & Development, CAD Modeling, Advanced Manufacturing and Rapid Prototyping techniques like Additive Manufacturing, which is known as 3D Printing, Lean Six Sigma, Advanced Data Analysis, and a few skills of industrial automation like PLC & HMI/SCADA Systems. I believe that all the industrial experiences coupled with my academics have helped me to augment my project management, problem-solving, communication skills, and collaborative skills in the fast-paced environment.
